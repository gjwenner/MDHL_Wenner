General Information

The Mighty Ducks Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play hockey. To be a member of MDHL, you must be between the ages of 4 - 12 and reside a Chicago northside neighborhood.
